Helmet of Salvation

"Take the helmet of salvation." — Ephesians 6:17

What it protects / does

Your mind — your thoughts, beliefs, and identity in Christ.

The Enemy's Tactic

He attacks your thoughts first. Doubt, shame, condemnation, confusion.

The Truth

You are saved. You are chosen. You are sealed by the Holy Spirit.

Activation Prayer

"Father, I put on the helmet of salvation. I declare that my mind belongs to You. I reject every thought that contradicts Your truth about who I am. In Jesus' name, Amen."
