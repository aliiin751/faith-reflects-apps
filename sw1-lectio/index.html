Lectio Divina
📖

1. Lectio — Read

Read the passage slowly, out loud if possible. Let the words wash over you. Don't rush or analyze yet; just listen.

🔍

2. Meditatio — Reflect

Read it again. What single word or short phrase immediately stands out to you? Sit with it. Turn it over in your mind.

🙏

3. Oratio — Respond

Talk to God about what you've read. What is He saying to you through this word? How does it apply to your life right now?

🌿

4. Contemplatio — Rest

Be silent. Rest in God's presence. You don't need to say anything anymore. Just be.
